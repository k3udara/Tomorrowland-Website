Feel it to the CORE

Open from the early afternoon, keen daytime clubbers will find their way to Tomorrowland's world-famous CORE stage which returns to the Ella. DJs including Joris Voorn and Purple Disco Machine will play the best techno, both classic and new while you either fist pump, jump or do some pre-ski warm-up stretches.

Open the Cage to madness

Cage turns into the best underground techno club in the French Alps on Thursday and Friday. On Thursday you can expect melodic house and techno and on Friday there's proper and heavy techno. Skilful Electronic Dance Music selectors at Cage include Enrico Sangiuliano, Coone, Mano Le Tough and Mark with a K.

Future Four

Nobody can predict the future, but there are some artists so fizzing with potential that their success seems predestined. Every fourth Tuesday of the month at 21:00 IST. In an exclusive one-hour show 4 unique artists will be selected and given an opportunity to showcase their talent. Discover them all in every Tuesday! Keep in touch!
